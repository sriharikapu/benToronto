We've got what you need!

benToronto is a member of the Blockchain Education Network. BEN is made up of students and alumni across the world who are creating bitcoin and blockchain clubs on their academic campuses. By exploring this socioeconomic experiment within the network of their peers, an organic environment of development can flourish with the human capital of students and alumni as the catalyst for innovation. In aggregate, the combined effort of all these clubs creates a rich web of interconnected blockchain hubs across the world. Impact global evolution through your local initiatives.
